## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This paper presents a smart-cushion approach for assessing activity level in people with sedentary lifestyles. It uses embedded sensing in the seat context as an alternative to body-worn or camera-based monitoring.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports related work on non-wearable seat-based sensing systems.
