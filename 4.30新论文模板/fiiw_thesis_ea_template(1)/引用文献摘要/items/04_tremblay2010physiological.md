## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This review examines physiological and health implications of sedentary lifestyles. It emphasizes that sedentary behavior is not simply the absence of exercise and may have distinct biological and public-health consequences.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the health-risk framing for prolonged sitting and the distinction between sedentary behavior and insufficient physical activity.
