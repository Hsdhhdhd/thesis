# 08. healy2008breaks

- Type: `article`
- Year: 2008
- Title: Breaks in sedentary time: beneficial associations with metabolic risk
- Venue/Publisher: Diabetes Care
- DOI: 10.2337/dc07-2046
- URL: None
- Verification link: https://doi.org/10.2337/dc07-2046

## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This study examines interruptions in sedentary time and metabolic-risk markers. It reports that more frequent breaks in sedentary time are associated with more favorable metabolic profiles, independent of total sedentary time.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the rationale for detecting prolonged sitting and encouraging movement breaks.
