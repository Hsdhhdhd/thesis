## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This early work describes a sensing chair based on pressure-distribution sensors. The system uses contact pressure patterns to infer seated posture or sitting-related states.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the history of chair- and pressure-sensor approaches to sitting monitoring.
