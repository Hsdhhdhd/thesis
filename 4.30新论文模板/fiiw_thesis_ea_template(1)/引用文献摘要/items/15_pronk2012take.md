## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This workplace intervention study evaluates the Take-a-Stand project, which aimed to reduce occupational sitting time and improve worker health. It reports changes in sitting behavior and related workplace health indicators after the intervention.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Provides an example of occupational sitting reduction as an applied intervention.
