## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This paper summarizes evidence considered by the 2018 Physical Activity Guidelines Advisory Committee on sedentary behavior and health. It covers associations between sedentary time and major health outcomes and emphasizes the relevance of total sitting time and activity replacement.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports broad health-outcome claims about sedentary behavior.
