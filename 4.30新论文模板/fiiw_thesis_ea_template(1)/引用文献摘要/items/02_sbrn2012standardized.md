## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This letter argues for standardized use of the terms sedentary and sedentary behaviours. It addresses inconsistent terminology in the field and provides an early shared definition based on low energy expenditure in sitting or reclining postures.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the historical basis for the sedentary-behavior definition.
