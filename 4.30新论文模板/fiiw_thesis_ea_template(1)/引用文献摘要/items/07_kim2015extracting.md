## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This paper discusses how sedentary behavior can be estimated from accelerometer data and highlights measurement considerations for research and surveillance. It addresses data-processing choices that affect objective sedentary-time estimates.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the use of accelerometer-derived estimates and the need for careful signal interpretation.
