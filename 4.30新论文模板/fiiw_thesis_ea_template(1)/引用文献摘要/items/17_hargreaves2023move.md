## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This feasibility and pilot study evaluates the Move More @ Work intervention, encouraging employees to be active for two minutes after every thirty minutes of sitting. It reports feasibility and preliminary outcomes for a timed movement-break approach.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the design rationale for prompting sitting breaks.
