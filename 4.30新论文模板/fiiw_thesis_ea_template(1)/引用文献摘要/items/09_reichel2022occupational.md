## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This systematic review examines associations between occupational sitting, cardiovascular outcomes, and cardiometabolic risk factors with attention to sex- and gender-sensitive evidence. It reports a nuanced evidence base rather than a single uniform risk estimate.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports a cautious health-background discussion of occupational sitting risks.
