## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This scoping review examines wearable-technology-mediated biofeedback for modulating spine motor control. It maps feedback modalities, target populations, and evidence gaps in wearable spine-related feedback systems.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports discussion of wearable feedback and the current lack of deep validation for haptic guidance.
