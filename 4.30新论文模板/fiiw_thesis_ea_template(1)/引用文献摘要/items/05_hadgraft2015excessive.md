## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This study investigates correlates of occupational sitting and television viewing among working adults. It shows that excessive sitting occurs across work and home contexts and is associated with demographic, occupational, and behavioral factors.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the practical relevance of workplace sitting as a target context.
