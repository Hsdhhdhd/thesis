## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This update to the Compendium of Physical Activities assigns metabolic equivalent values to a wide range of activities. It provides standardized MET intensity codes that are widely used to classify activities by energy expenditure.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports the thesis statement that sedentary behavior is defined by low energy expenditure, especially the 1.5 MET threshold.
