## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This consensus paper standardizes sedentary-behavior terminology. It defines sedentary behavior as waking behavior with energy expenditure of 1.5 METs or lower while sitting, reclining, or lying, and separates sedentary behavior from related concepts such as physical inactivity and stationary behavior.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Use this as the primary definitional source for sedentary behavior in the introduction and background.
