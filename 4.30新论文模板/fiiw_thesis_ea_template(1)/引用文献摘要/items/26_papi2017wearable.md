## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This systematic review examines wearable technologies for assessing spine movement. It reviews sensor types, validation approaches, target movements, and practical limitations in wearable spine assessment.

Supports the thesis positioning of wearable IMU-based posture and spine-related sensing.
