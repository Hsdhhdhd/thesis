## Non-verbatim Abstract Summary

This systematic review evaluates evidence on active workstations and their effects on workplace productivity and performance. It considers whether alternatives to conventional seated work can reduce sedentary behavior without harming work output.

## Relevance to This Thesis

Supports comparison with workstation-based interventions.
